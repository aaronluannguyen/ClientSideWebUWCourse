Batman: The Dark Knight

Batman Origins

Bruce Wayne, as a child, was struck with the tragedy of having his parents murdered by street criminals in an alley in the city of Gotham. Ever since that day, he made a vow to fight injustice to honor his parents. He used his family fortunes from their family company, Wayne Enterprises, to travel around the world to master as many martial art forms as he could, learning the art of being a detective, and many other skills that would eventually lead him towards the hero he is today. He's widely-known as Batman, but has also donned many other names such as The Dark Knight, The Caped Crusader, and The World's Greatest Detective.

About Batman

Batman is known for his expertise in hand to hand combat as well as being an expert strategist and his seemingly unlimited amount of resources that grant him amazing new gadgets and vehicles. He is also recognized for being a superhero with no true powers, and strives toward proving that anyone can be a hero, even if you don't have powers. He is driven by his vow as a young child when his parents were brutally murdered in front of him. He has a strict moral code which drives him to serve justice without killing a person. Batman's most famous villain or foe is The Joker and their rivalry is widely recognized across all story arcs that he is involved in.

Batman Appearances

Batman is a comic character from DC comics. Since being introduced by comic books, Batman has made appearances in TV shows and movies as well. He has been featured in many different story arcs and alternate universes in both the comics, TV series, and movies. He is also famously known for being a part of the DC Trinity along with Superman and Wonder Woman, as well as being one of the founding members of the Justice League.
