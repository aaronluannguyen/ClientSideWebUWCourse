Background
Bruce Wayne, as a child, was struck with the tragedy of having his parents murdered by street criminals in an alley in the city of Gotham. Ever since that day, he made a vow to fight injustice to honor his parents. He used his family fortunes from their family company, Wayne Enterprises, to travel around the world to master as many martial art forms as he could, learning the art of being a detective, and many other skills that would eventually lead him towards the hero he is today. He's widely-known as Batman, but has also donned many other names such as The Dark Knight, The Caped Crusader, and The World's Greatest Detective. His abilities are listed as being an master martial artist, combat strategist, having inexhaustible wealth, having a brilliant deductive skill, having a genius-level intelligence, being a master detective, being a master escapologist, having peak human physical condition, and having access to high tech equipment.
